Tingtagel

The word "Tingtagel" means village on a mountain and the villge of Tingtagel in Cornwall in the South West of England, is situated on the Atlantic coast, high above the sea.

The Castle

Tintagel Castle is said to have been the birthplace of the legandary King Arthur.

In Geoffrey's Historia, Gorlois, Duke of Cornwall, puts his wife Igraine in Tintagel Castle while he is at war.

Merlin disguised Uther Pendragon as Gorlois so that Uther could enter Tintagel and impregnate Igraine while pretending to be Gorlois.

Uther and Igraine's child was King Arthur.
